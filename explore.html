Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a property?

Simply browse our properties, select your preferred accommodation, choose your dates, and complete the secure booking process. You'll receive instant confirmation via email.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by property. Most bookings can be cancelled free of charge up to 24-48 hours before check-in. Check the specific property's cancellation terms during booking.

Are all properties verified?

Yes, all properties listed on Stayscape undergo a thorough verification process including physical inspections, document verification, and quality assessments to ensure they meet our standards.

Do you offer customer support?

We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. Our support team is always ready to assist you with any questions or issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and popular digital payment methods. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.
